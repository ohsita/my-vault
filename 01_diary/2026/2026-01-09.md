Using the onboard microscope, we conduct microbial searches. This compact, lightweight microimaging device enables µm-level observation without manual focus adjustment.

We directly image aqueous mixtures of soil and solvent, then use image analysis software such as ImageJ to identify areas potentially harboring microorganisms. Combining these analysis results with those from other detection devices provides a more accurate assessment of the potential for life.


搭載する顕微鏡を用いて微生物の探索を実施する。この小型軽量のマイクロイメージングデバイスは手動の焦点調節無しでµｍレベルの観察が可能である。
